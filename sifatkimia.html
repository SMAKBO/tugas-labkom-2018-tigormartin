Chemical Properties of Stearic Acid

Chemical Properties:

  • 1. Pure stearic acid is a waxy substance.

  • 2. No smell .

  • 3. Often in the form of white candles or yellow flakes.

  • 4. When heated, it becomes a clear liquid.

  • 5.Easy to hydrogenate.
